Company Profile

JC Premiere Business International

JC Premiere Business International is a direct selling company based in the Philippines which is founded by Jonathan So and Carlito Macadangdang. It has branched out to Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Qatar, Italy, Taiwan, Singapore, Hongkong, Malaysia and Oman as part of its growing presence in the global market.

JC Premiere revolutionized the industry when it offered a unique combination of an Online Franchise business carrying the food concept, delivery service and an online mall.

Our Mission To be an advocate of health and wellness by providing dependable food supplements to consumers. To be able to provide a system that will make our products within reach of every household and provide them the healthy lifestyle that they deserve. To promote a home-based business to each household. To enrich the lives of our independent distributors (both full-time and part-time) by offering the most equitable compensation plans. To nurture a working environment anchored on harmony, trust, and respect on the diversity and individuality of men. To gain the admiration of our employees, independent distributors and the community not only for what we have achieved but the means through which we achieved them.

To be one of the leading companies globally recognized to provide quality and innovative food products and supplements for the Filipino and the rest of the world.
